About Me

Hi, I’m Raghav Solanki, currently pursuing Computer Science Engineering. Beyond code and algorithms, I find myself drawn to the art of storytelling through photography. What started as a casual hobby soon became a way for me to explore perspectives, emotions, and creativity. I love experimenting with different styles — portraits, landscapes, and candid street shots. Each photo I take is not just about freezing a moment, but about seeing the world from a fresh angle. Being part of Opticlick is my step toward growing as a photographer while learning from others. With my tech background, I also explore the intersection of AI and art, aiming to merge creativity with innovation in the future.
